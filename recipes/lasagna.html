Lasagna

Description

A pasta dish with noodles, meat, cheese, and sauce. Ingredients are layered in a baking dish and loaded baked in an oven.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Make cheese mixture
  3. Boil pasta
  4. Layer pasta, meat sauce, and cheese in baking dish
  5. Bake
